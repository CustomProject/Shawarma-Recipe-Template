Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon sweet paprika
  • ½ teaspoon oregano

For the Sauce (Optional)

  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice

For Assembly(Optional)

  • 1 whole wheat pita or tortilla
  • ½ cucumber (diced)
  • 1 tomato (diced)
  • ½ red onion (sliced thinly)
  • Lettuce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preparing the Chicken

    2. Slice the chicken breast into thin strips and season with salt, pepper, paprika, and oregano.
    Heat a pan over medium heat and add the olive oil.
    Cook the chicken, stirring occasionally, until golden and fully cooked (about 7-10 minutes).

  2. Making the Sauce

    3. Mix the Greek yogurt with the minced garlic and lemon juice.

  3. Assembling the Shawarma

    4. Warm the pita or tortilla for a few seconds.
    Spread the yogurt sauce over the bread.
    Add the cooked chicken and chopped vegetables.
    Wrap it up and serve immediately.
